About Me

  • Dr. Weite Zhang has been a Senior Systems Engineer at Analog Devices since March 2023. Previously, he was a Senior Research Engineer at Northeastern University. His research and work focus on advancing wireless communication and sensing systems, encompassing system architecture design, hardware schematics, embedded firmware/software, and integration testing and measurement.
  • Dr. Weite Zhang earned his Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from Northeastern University, Boston, MA, USA, in May 2022, his M.S. in Electronics and Science Technology from Zhejiang University, Hangzhou, China, in March 2017, and his B.S. in Electronics and Science Technology from Zhejiang University of Technology, Hangzhou, China, in June 2014.
