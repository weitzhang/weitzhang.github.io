About Me

  • I have been a Senior Research Engineer at Northeastern University since May 2022, where I work on multimodal sensor fusion and artificial intelligence.
  • I achieved the PhD Degree in Electrical Engineering at Northeastern University in May 2022, where I worked on millimeter-wave imaging systems and RF/mmWave antennas and circuits in Professor Jose Martinez's Lab. I obtained the MS Degree in Electronics Science and Technology at Zhejiang University in March 2017, supervised by Professor Xianmin Zhang, where I worked on MIMO wireless communication systems with orbital angular momemtum (OAM) multiplexing, software-defined radios, and microwave OAM antennas. I had my BS Degree in Electronics Science and Technology at Zhejiang University of Technology in June 2014.
