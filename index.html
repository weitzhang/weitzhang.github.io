About Me

  • Dr. Weite Zhang has been a Senior SW System Engineer at Analog Devices since March 2023. Before that, he was a Senior Research Engineer at Northeastern University.
  • He obtained the Ph.D. Degree in Electrical Engineering from Northeastern University, Boston, MA, USA, in May 2022, the M.S. Degree in Electronics and Science Technology from Zhejiang University, Hangzhou, China, in March 2017, and the B.S. Degree in Electronics and Science Technology from Zhejiang University of Technology, Hangzhou, China, in June 2014.
